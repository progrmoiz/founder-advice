# DO: Go Organic First, Paid Ads Second

> Prove the message works organically before spending money on ads.

**7 founders** weighed in on this. **8 total mentions** across 7 interviews.

## At $50K-100K/mo

**Jacob** — Faceless Video (SaaS, $1M ARR)
> "To have success as a B2C app you need to have viral potential your CAC customer acquisition costs it's pretty low and so to spend a lot on ads or other methods of promotion is not really viable for a a B2C app"

## At $10K-50K/mo

**Steven** — Puff Count (mobile app, $44K/mo)
> "Your first campaign cannot be a purchase campaign you can't optimize for those users just quite yet because TikTok and Facebook don't really know who those users are yet."

**Mickey** — Late Social Media API (SaaS, $40K/mo)
> "We use automated bidding which sets a target CPA at $120 which is our average CAC threshold. Google's algorithm optimizes bids to hit this target."

**Mal Baron** — Prayer Lock (mobile app, $21K/mo)
> "I created a super broad campaign without any audience controls nothing. I only specified the location to US and inserted a bunch of my top UGC girl reaction videos from before."

**Jack Ficks** — Curiosity Quench and PostBridge (mobile app and SaaS, $10,000/month combined)
> "I think anyone can get thousands of downloads to their app or visitors to the website just with one hour a day of marketing with no ad spend"

**Ivan** — Lancer (SaaS, $10K/month)
> "Instead of going out to them directly using paid ads or cold email at scale I identify the layer above them basically a profile of user who has access to them. I call them connectors and essentially convince them to be affiliates."

## At Unknown

**Lots** — Multiple apps (Bible Buddy, Magic Music, Toxic Traits, Pray Screen) (mobile app, $500,000 total from 4 app sales)
> "Once I have the right hook then I launch it on meta ads and that way I actually get really low CPIs. For pray screen we were getting between 30 to 50 cents cost per install in the US market which is like unheard of."
