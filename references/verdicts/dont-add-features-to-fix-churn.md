# DON'T: Add Features to Fix Churn

> If people leave, it's not because you lack features. It's because the core isn't solving their problem.

**8 founders** weighed in on this. **10 total mentions** across 8 interviews.

## At $100K+/mo

**Gam Mubes** — lemlist (SaaS, $30M ARR)
> "coded product quickly to ship features without considering scale (cost: 1.5 years to rebuild entire architecture). Lesson: had to rebuild entirely when user scale didn't match development approach"

## At $50K-100K/mo

**Mark Lou** — ShipFast (SaaS, $80K/mo)
> "Building vitamin apps instead of painkiller apps (cost: Multiple failed products that didn't generate significant revenue). Lesson: Focus on solving actual problems people will pay for, not just nice-to-have features"

## At $10K-50K/mo

**Aayal and Yali** — PropGPT (mobile app, $30K/mo)
> "Built first version that had good trial conversion but terrible paid conversion (cost: 5 months of initial development plus 4 months of rebuilding). Lesson: If your product is shit nobody's going to buy it - need to be extremely humble about your product"

**Nick** — Block to Pin (SaaS, $16K/mo)
> "I take a deep care about everyone using the app. I review screen recordings of what they do when something happens with their account. When they need any feature I just build it in a few hours or days and people really feel it when you care."

**Chris** — Wish List (mobile app, $150K/year)
> "Every time I shipped a feature or just fixed a bug for someone specific user I would text these users 'Hi John I just fixed your issue' or 'Hi John I just implemented the feature you requested could you let me know how you feel about it.'"

**Demitro** — Screenshot One (SaaS, $12K/mo)
> "I started reaching out through emails I was checking their profile their company what was their use case and then I was like saying you know one yes no question did you churn because of let's say pricing and people started to answer."

**Joseph and Teemo** — Setter AI (SaaS, $10K/mo)
> "We have focused more on the higher paying businesses because we saw lower churn with these"

## At $5K-10K/mo

**Pauline** — AI Crea (SaaS, $8K/mo)
> "I really reduced churn drastically by adding yearly plan. Now they are engaged for a year so they will use the service better. I did a free trial so people can try before subscribing."
