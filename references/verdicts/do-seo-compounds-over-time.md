# DO: SEO Compounds — Start Early

> SEO takes months to kick in but becomes your best channel once it does.

**6 founders** weighed in on this. **11 total mentions** across 6 interviews.

## At $100K+/mo

**Tim Stoddard** — Stazzi Internet Marketing (agency, $5.2-5.5M/yr)
> "You don't have to build this giant media entity like you can tackle it one keyword one article at a time. The trick is to be methodical about it."

**Charlie Chang** — Charlie Chang YouTube Channels + Paired Staffing (content site, $200-250K/mo)
> "I prefer to go the searchable route in terms of videos that perform extremely well"

**Nico** — Neuroframes (SaaS, $100K/mo)
> "Dedicated landing pages free tools definitely still work really well on Google."

## At $50K-100K/mo

**Alex** — Finn Versus (affiliate marketing website, $1M/year)
> "If you're building a business that relies on SEO you want to go after low competition longtail keywords"

## At $10K-50K/mo

**Aayush** — Elephas (SaaS, $150K/year)
> "Anything with a keyword difficulty of less than 20 and a search volume of greater than 500 is a very good combination especially if you are like a new website you don't have a lot of domain authority."

**Vikash** — Bulk Mockup (SaaS, $12K/mo)
> "If you create a YouTube video with the same search intent and optimize that for Google your YouTube videos will rank and 22% views on our YouTube channel comes from Google search."
