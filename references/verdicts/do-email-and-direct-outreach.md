# DO: Cold Email and Direct Outreach Still Works

> DMs, cold emails, and direct outreach get your first customers faster than content.

**14 founders** weighed in on this. **18 total mentions** across 14 interviews.

## At $100K+/mo

**David Park** — Jenny AI (SaaS, $10M/yr)
> "We don't only just send emails you got to remember that a lot of these people making content they they're not business people of their own they're just normal people They don't check their emails every day like Founders do so instead of sending email emails we send DMS as well"

**Blake Anderson** — Riz GPT, Umax, CI (mobile app, $10M total across 3 apps)
> "I would find that one of them had a Discord link hidden in their Instagram bio and I would join that Discord and I would send a message every 10 minutes until the person responded there were guys that I was dming their mom saying hey get me a contact with your son I want to pay him money"

**Pat Walls** — Starter Story (content site, $1.8M/year)
> "Really simple stuff like having a welcome sequence when someone signs up to your email list you send one two three four five different emails and tell a story in your emails"

## At $50K-100K/mo

**Marcos** — The Birdhouse (agency, $70K/mo)
> "I was sending out 100 200 DMS a day had it all in my notion following up and that took about a few weeks before I kind of really landed some interest"

**Lara Costa** — Cleo (SaaS, $60K/month)
> "Build an email list you never know when you're going to lose access to your social media platforms you never know when you'll need to actually promote something so it's better to actually build and then nurture that list"

**Scott** — None (productized service, $50K/mo)
> "A cold email looks like it's basically it's really simple it's basically two sentences it's like hey you know we noticed on your site that you don't have a video like above the fold on your landing page that's something we do like have you ever looked into that and that's it"

## At $10K-50K/mo

**Aayal and Yali** — PropGPT (mobile app, $30K/mo)
> "The smartest way to grow today is through influencer marketing - their followers engage deeply and convert faster when they see someone that they admire using your product."

**Gil** — Subscribr (SaaS, $30K/mo)
> "You've got to set up an email list and start communicating with people at least once a week. Your email list is really where you're building that trust."

**Anish** — Save Wise (SaaS, $25K/mo)
> "Work with the admins of the groups the moderators of the group to figure out how to get a top level post when your product is ready."

**George Lampropolis** — Wrestle AI (mobile app, $17K/mo)
> "Step one if you're just starting out dm 100 people a day"

**Nick** — Block to Pin (SaaS, $16K/mo)
> "Try different marketing angles outreach to people on X Reddit via email record YouTube videos do everything you can to reach out to your target audience and build features to stand out for them."

**Lewis** — Audio Pen (SaaS, $15K/mo)
> "Build in public tell people what you're building tell them what it'll look like see what they like about it see what they don't like about it perhaps if you're seeing a lot of people resonate with what you've built spin up a quick email list even if it's just a simple Google form."

## At $5K-10K/mo

**Pauline** — AI Crea (SaaS, $8K/mo)
> "Think about how you can reach your customers. Don't think about Reddit messages or Discord messages or DM people. Think about really how you could build genuine connection to your customers."

## At Unknown

**Will Cannon** — Uplead and Signaturely (SaaS, $30M+ total sales)
> "You don't need long emails you need short ones that feel human and I think any business can take this, they can tweak those lines and they can send out the same cold email that we did."
