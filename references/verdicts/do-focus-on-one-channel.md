# DO: Master One Channel Before Adding More

> Founders who win go deep on one channel before spreading thin.

**66 founders** weighed in on this. **91 total mentions** across 66 interviews.

## At $100K+/mo

**Ryan Chen** — Neuro (e-commerce, $10 million/month)
> "At the end of the day it's like who do you want to go go into battle with and like I want to go into battle with my best friend because I trust him"

**Saba Kenned** — V.io (SaaS, $40M/yr)
> "Once we got back to the UK after California I did one thing which was only focus on growth Like the only thing I had to do was make traffic higher than the day or the week before and make revenue higher than the day or the week before"

**Gam Mubes** — lemlist (SaaS, $30M ARR)
> "growing a company is never an exponential curve It's Always A S curve that can look like an exponential curve but there is always a plateau and the only companies that have like a proper exponential curve are the one planning for the next S curve"

**Casey Holiday** — Qalo (e-commerce, $30M)
> "It was always focused on being better as a company not bigger"

**Ian Meers** — Oceans (agency, $15M/yr)
> "In that initial stretch even though I wanted great you know easy lifestyle I knew the first 20 the first 50 you got to really lean in you got to be there you got to pick up the phone you got to call people and you just got to go beyond the bounds of what you might do when the company's large."

**Colin** — Sheets & Giggles (e-commerce, $1M/month)
> "I had a one-on-one connection with thousands of people I answered every single social media comment myself I responded to every customer care email myself"

**David Park** — Jenny AI (SaaS, $10M/yr)
> "How we think about growth at the highest level is just having more users today versus yesterday and having more users tomorrow versus today"

**Greg Eisenberg** — Late Checkout (holding company, $10M+/year)
> "The ACP funnel is the secret sauce that I've used for the last 15 years. A stands for audience, C stands for Community, P for product."
