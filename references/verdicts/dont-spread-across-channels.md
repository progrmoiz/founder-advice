# DON'T: Spread Across Too Many Channels

> Doing 5 channels at 20% beats doing 1 channel at 100%. Wait — it doesn't.

**4 founders** weighed in on this. **5 total mentions** across 4 interviews.

## At $50K-100K/mo

**Ure** — Rummer, Parakeet AI, Optivase, Parakeet AI Apply Agent (multiple (hardware device, SaaS, B2B SaaS, AI agent), $60K/mo)
> "Spent a lot of money on Facebook ads and Google ads (cost: significant money). Lesson: Focus on organic short form content instead of paid ads"

## At $10K-50K/mo

**Angus Chang** — Bank Statement Converter (SaaS, $40K/mo)
> "spending money on Google ads that were never profitable (cost: spending $1000 on ads to bring in $300 in sales over 6 months). Lesson: focus on organic growth and product improvement instead of paid ads"

**Mal Baron** — Prayer Lock (mobile app, $21K/mo)
> "Optimized for downloads instead of trials in first paid ads attempt (cost: Failed paid ads campaign and wasted ad spend). Lesson: Need to optimize for the right metric and have good onboarding conversion rate first"

**Joseph and Teemo** — Setter AI (SaaS, $10K/mo)
> "Tried paid ads (cost: Reduced margins from 90% to 80%). Lesson: Focus on what's already working (organic channels)"
