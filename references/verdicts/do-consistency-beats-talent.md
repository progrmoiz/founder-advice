# DO: Consistency Beats Talent

> Show up every day. The founders who win are the ones who don't stop.

**20 founders** weighed in on this. **27 total mentions** across 20 interviews.

## At $100K+/mo

**Oliver Brocato** — Tabs Chocolate (e-commerce, $11M/yr)
> "It's like every day you're compounding compounding compounding and then one day you wake up and you know you're worth millions of dollars and that's kind of how it is."

**Dicky Bush** — Ship 30 for 30 (online course, $10M/year)
> "Wake up at 4:30 a.m. write for 2 hours get back to my desk at 7:00 a.m. do my black rock work during the day log off as soon as I could and then build in the evenings"

**Greg Eisenberg** — Late Checkout (holding company, $10M+/year)
> "I plan out what I want to do today what are the two or three things that if I did these things today I would be happy."

**Sebastian Georgu** — None (SaaS, $250K/mo)
> "At one point when you can sustainably live off the income from the business double down on that and go all in on the business it's going to be hard for people because it's more of a long-term play versus a short-term play."

**David Park** — Jenny AI (SaaS, $3M/yr)
> "You need that discipline I think if you really care about your dream and if you really care about making something that is used by millions of people people then you can't give it like a onewe shot."

**Charlie Chang** — Charlie Chang YouTube Channels + Paired Staffing (content site, $200-250K/mo)
> "For anyone that thinks they're in a rut or they want to have a big breakthrough I'd say one thing they can do is really change their environment or change their routine like completely"

**Andre Heckle Jr** — List Kit (SaaS, $200K/mo)
> "It's a game of staying very consistent over a long period of time and when times are not good get even more consistent and get even more intentional about your action items."

**Justin** — Knowledge entrepreneur portfolio (content site, $1.7M/yr)
> "You can't just wake up every day as a oneperson business and say well what do I do on Monday what do I do on Tuesday what do I do on Wednesday when I wake up every morning during the week I know exactly what I'm doing."

## At $50K-100K/mo

**Marcos** — The Birdhouse (agency, $70K/mo)
> "Play to your strengths and have the discipline to just stick it out because I think especially at first being an introvert I used to try to do things I wasn't good at sales video streaming I tried all of it if I played to my strengths which is more introverted writing stuff like this I think that would have been a huge help"

**Ure** — Rummer, Parakeet AI, Optivase, Parakeet AI Apply Agent (multiple (hardware device, SaaS, B2B SaaS, AI agent), $60K/mo)
> "Every day I wake up and I kind of think about what is the most effective thing I can do to push my business forward"

## At $10K-50K/mo

**Dominico** — Multiple apps (Pitch 2.0, OneUp.ai, YourCoverLetter.com, RecapAI.com, QuitsBot.io, WebDesigner.io, SoftGen.ai) (SaaS, $500K ARR)
> "I use different technique I would say the most useful one is the pomodoro technique where you break your work into focused intervals and typically 25 minutes followed by very short breaks."

**Andy Cloak** — Data Fetcher (SaaS, $23K/mo)
> "The biggest lesson has been the power of focus over chasing shiny objects. Over the few years I've wasted probably 6 months trying to launch side businesses."

**Demitro** — Screenshot One (SaaS, $12K/mo)
> "If you're a solopreneur especially you need to take care of your mental health because it's like it's your only tool that you use every day and if you feel bad your quality of decisions are also much much worse."

## At $5K-10K/mo

**Romsri** — Supermeme and Question.ai (SaaS, $8,500/month)

## At $1K-5K/mo

**Pat Walls** — Starter Story (content site, $3,500/mo)
