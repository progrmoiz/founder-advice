# DO: Validate Before Building

> Talk to customers, check demand, get paid before writing code.

**97 founders** weighed in on this. **174 total mentions** across 97 interviews.

## At $100K+/mo

**Ryan Chen** — Neuro (e-commerce, $10 million/month)
> "We would see the number of repeat buyers and the percentage of repeat buyers as a percent of Revenue so we knew that we had some type of base"

**Saba Kenned** — V.io (SaaS, $40M/yr)
> "The first thing you need to work out how to do is actually are we capable of putting something together. The first thing you've got to do is actually go through the cycle once of hey we bought a domain Cool We now to make a back end great We know how to make a landing page Perfect"

**Casey Holiday** — Qalo (e-commerce, $30M)
> "You should be able to communicate to anyone as an entrepreneur this is the problem that I'm solving start there then to determine what the product is to solve that problem"

**Ian Meers** — Oceans (agency, $15M/yr)
> "Go find something that people already want and do it differently or better and there's your equation for a successful business."

**Colin** — Sheets & Giggles (e-commerce, $1M/month)
> "I really encourage people to build a business model first that they really feel strongly about that makes sense for them their skill set what they're best at in the world. I think business model first product second"

**Aaron Spivak** — Hush Blankets (e-commerce, $48M in 4 years)
> "Why don't we just call everyone because clearly they were buying it they're not buying it now why are they not buying well just ask"

**David Park** — Jenny AI (SaaS, $10M/yr)
> "You can explicitly ask them for their Instagram handles and you can just check who they're following what do they enjoy watching in their free time"

**Greg Eisenberg** — Late Checkout (holding company, $10M+/year)
