# DON'T: Ignore Distribution

> If you build it, they won't come. Distribution is not optional.

**3 founders** weighed in on this. **3 total mentions** across 3 interviews.

## At $100K+/mo

**Dustin** — Magi AI (SaaS, almost $100,000 a month)
> "Two product ideas that completely failed to launch and crashed and burned (cost: confidence and time). Lesson: Don't let past failures define you"

## At $50K-100K/mo

**Mark Lou** — ShipFast (SaaS, $80K/mo)
> "Built AI startup for 6 months with no customers, focused only on product not marketing (cost: 6 months, ended up broke living in 12 square meters with cockroaches). Lesson: Need to focus on getting customers, not just building product"

## At $10K-50K/mo

**Nick** — Block to Pin (SaaS, $16K/mo)
> "Expected viral launch but nothing happened (cost: 10 days waiting for first customer). Lesson: Can't rely on viral launches, need to focus on solving real problems for real people"
