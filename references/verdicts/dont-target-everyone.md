# DON'T: Try to Target Everyone

> A product for everyone is a product for no one. Go narrow, go deep.

**10 founders** weighed in on this. **11 total mentions** across 10 interviews.

## At $100K+/mo

**Gam Mubes** — lemlist (SaaS, $30M ARR)
> "we talk a lot about blue ocean and red ocean so I started with like this blue ocean idea because no one was on it but if we have competitors it means that there is always a product Market fit these guys are making shit tons of money so all you have to do is to be better"

**Aaron Spivak** — Hush Blankets (e-commerce, $48M in 4 years)
> "Pivoted from Regular weighted blankets that were too hot in summer to Cooling weighted blankets with custom ice fabric. Why: Customer calls revealed everyone loved the blanket but stopped using it because they were sweating. Impact: went from $0 sales to $1M Kickstarter"

**Greg Eisenberg** — Late Checkout (holding company, $10M+/year)
> "Listening to top entrepreneurs who said to build a venture-backed company and scale like Mark Zuckerberg (cost: 12-13 years working 7am to 2am every single day, 7 days a week). Lesson: I was living someone else's life by doing that. Everyone deep down knows what's for them and life is about getting to that truth."

**Sebastian Georgu** — None (SaaS, $250K/mo)
> "hired a lot of developers that weren't very good (cost: time and money). Lesson: we didn't know what we were doing but every time we made a good hire the boat lifted for everyone"

**Mike** — Multiple SaaS apps (curator.io, frill.co, juno.co, fluke.co, soonmile.co) (SaaS, $200K/mo)
> "We will never go after an AI focused business too many times you have an idea that relies on something an API that you do not control something that you're not in control of which puts you at massive massive risk."

**Nico** — Neuroframes (SaaS, $100K/mo)
> "niching down was a huge unlock for us actually. You're a musician You come to a site it says text to video for everyone or AI video generator and then you already need to do a mental step. versus you come to a site it says bam this is the platform to create music videos."

## At $50K-100K/mo

**Alex** — Finn Versus (affiliate marketing website, $1M/year)
> "You can't win in a crowded space when you're young and new and your site isn't authoritative on any level but you can win queries that nobody has ever written something for"

**Adam Liddell** — Portfolio of mobile apps (mobile app, $50-60K/mo)
> "being too focused on business and neglecting family (cost: lost relationships and family). Lesson: balance is important, don't let everyone fall into line for your business goals"

## At $10K-50K/mo

**Andy Cloak** — Data Fetcher (SaaS, $23K/mo)
> "I probably not building on like chatpt or claude. Although there's like huge growth in those tools everyone and their dog is building for Chat GPT and Claude just because there's so much competition."

**Nick** — Block to Pin (SaaS, $16K/mo)
> "Your app should be the best one for one specific person. It will be absolute disaster for everyone else."
