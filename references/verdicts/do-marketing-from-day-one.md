# DO: Start Marketing Before the Product is Ready

> Marketing is not a phase — it's a habit. Start before you ship.

**99 founders** weighed in on this. **285 total mentions** across 99 interviews.

## At $100K+/mo

**Saba Kenned** — V.io (SaaS, $40M/yr)
> "Because I had no money It was only like SEO. People search for things like trim video crop video There's actually about 500 of these search terms So I built a landing page for every single one of those things And then I'd record YouTube videos for every single landing page It was just brute force"

**Gam Mubes** — lemlist (SaaS, $30M ARR)
> "the magnet Persona is the Persona that's going to attract a lot more customers and if you take for example Apple their only focus for years was designers because designers are cool so if you have a product that makes people feel like they are amongst this magnet Persona then it's awesome"

**Ian Meers** — Oceans (agency, $15M/yr)
> "I from the beginning knew that referrals were going to be the secret to our growth if you have a great service business they usually grow through Word of Mouth."

**Lane Wagner** — boot.dev (SaaS, $1M/mo)
> "Working with influencers was like a cheat code because the influencers are already trusted by their audience so if you can get an influencer to try your thing and like it you kind of unlock a new little section of the market."

**Colin** — Sheets & Giggles (e-commerce, $1M/month)
> "I sponsor podcasts that I love and I sponsor YouTube channels that I watch. Odds are the people who also like them are like you they're going to resonate with your personality"

**Aaron Spivak** — Hush Blankets (e-commerce, $48M in 4 years)
> "I think storytelling is the most important thing you can have when you're marketing a brand and storytelling is the emotions and it's the magic that people want to feel before they go out and purchase something"

**Oliver Brocato** — Tabs Chocolate (e-commerce, $11M/yr)
> "I try to make them seem as native as possible consumers are smart if they think it's an app they're not going to watch it so I make the videos like super on par with whatever's trending in the moment whatever sound is popping whatever like movement is happening."

**David Park** — Jenny AI (SaaS, $10M/yr)
> "To create a truly viral video you have to understand what your viewers want to see which you have to ask yourself like who are your viewers what are their interests what are they currently watching why are they watching it and how can you create content even more engaging than what they're currently watching"
