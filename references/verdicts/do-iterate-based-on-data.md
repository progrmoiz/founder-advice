# DO: Iterate Based on Data, Not Gut

> Let users and metrics tell you what to build next, not your instincts.

**21 founders** weighed in on this. **24 total mentions** across 21 interviews.

## At $100K+/mo

**Tibo** — Multiple SaaS Portfolio (SaaS, $700K/mo)
> "If you have a 90% failure rate and you want to take a year to build, it might take 9 years to get something that works. If you compress that to weeks, you reach success much faster."

**Anton** — Letterly (mobile app, $250K/mo)
> "The truth is you'll have to iterate and iterate a lot and iterate on different levels so the first iteration is on prototypes so you draw something and you begin to discuss it to try it out and you simplify you change"

**Charlie Chang** — Charlie Chang YouTube Channels + Paired Staffing (content site, $200-250K/mo)
> "I also think it's really important to be brainstorming and thinking about different potential pivots you can make"

**Justin** — Knowledge entrepreneur portfolio (content site, $1.7M/yr)
> "The way that I have leveled up is I'm a hypothesisdriven creator same thing I was when I was a sales leader in my my previous career which meant making a hypothesis testing it seeing if it worked if it did doing it twice as much or three times as much or five times as much and if it didn't completely eliminating it."

**Thomas Frank** — Ultimate Brain and Creators Companion (digital products, $100K-$120K/mo)
> "Do whatever you have to do to make sure you publish on the schedule and try to get just a little bit better at something interesting every single time you publish"

## At $10K-50K/mo

**Steven** — Puff Count (mobile app, $44K/mo)
> "If you can get to a 3:1 CAC to LTV ratio congratulations you've built a money printing machine."

**Aayal and Yali** — PropGPT (mobile app, $30K/mo)

**Alex Finn** — Creator Buddy (SaaS, $300K ARR)
> "If you don't know what to build just build something if you don't know what to tweet about just tweet anything one of two things will happen you'll either figure out what works or what doesn't work"

**Hassam** — Launch Fast (SaaS, $21.8K/mo)

**Maddox Schmidtoffer** — DuckMath (content site, $15K/mo)

**Max** — Portfolio of 28 mobile apps (mobile app, $10K/mo)
