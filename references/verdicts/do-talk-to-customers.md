# DO: Talk to Customers Obsessively

> The answers aren't in your head. They're in customer conversations.

**4 founders** weighed in on this. **8 total mentions** across 4 interviews.

## At $100K+/mo

**Tim Stoddard** — Stazzi Internet Marketing (agency, $5.2-5.5M/yr)
> "Learn how to ask questions so that the person tells you their pain points. I write down what the person says and respond with a solution to that problem."

## At $50K-100K/mo

**Marcos** — The Birdhouse (agency, $70K/mo)
> "Sales is really just about conviction so if you actually believe in what you're doing can actually help someone make more money it's pretty easy to sell"

## At $10K-50K/mo

**Colin Macintosh** — Sheets Resume (SaaS, $20K/mo)
> "Right now you can get open AI credits dirt cheap and I don't think it'll be like that forever they're just doing user acquisition right now on their end clearly right now is the best time to be building an AI."

**Thomas** — Unid (SaaS, $10,000/month)
> "If your project doesn't work in let's say January it doesn't mean it won't work in July so my advice here would be to stay consistent and don't give up because you don't have the right timing."
